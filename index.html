Our Story
Family Craft Butchers

A family trade,
seven generations deep

Sheehan’s Butchers is a family run business with a tradition and heritage going back to 1870, when the first Sheehan’s shop opened in Mallow. In 1962, Eugene Sheehan reopened the Mallow shop, and his four sons — Eugene Jnr, Colm, David and Edward — followed in his footsteps, growing the business to five locations across Cork City and North Cork.

Over the years, Sheehan Brothers have won numerous awards from the Craft Butchers’ Association of Ireland, in particular for their sausages and spiced beef — two products that have become something of a Cork institution. Today, with the seventh generation already stepping up, the craft continues.
